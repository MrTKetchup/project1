About me

History

I grew up in Wells, Somerset and graduated with an MEng in Chemical Engineering from the University of Birmingham in 2013. I now live in Bristol and have done since 2014.

Since graduating I have had a range of different careers varying from Nuclear Safety Consultant to Sous Chef to most recently as a Brewer at a small independent craft brewery located in Bath called Electric Bear Brewing Co.

I am now in the next chapter of my life, learning how to develop code and I am currently enrolled at the iO Academy Full Stack Track Bootcamp.
